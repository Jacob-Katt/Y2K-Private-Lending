What We Offer

We provide private lending options specifically structured to support renovation and investment projects across various property types. Whether you’re flipping a single-family residence, developing a duplex, or doing a ground-up build, our funding terms are designed to keep your project on track and your momentum strong.

  • 🔨 Fix & Flip Loans: Finance both the purchase and renovation of investment properties in a single loan, streamlining your flip process.
  • 🌉 Bridge Loans: Quick capital designed to secure opportunities fast or transition you into long-term solutions.
  • 💵 Cash-Out Refinance: Leverage completed projects to unlock equity and fund future investments.
  • 🏘️ Rental Property Loans: Structured for BRRRR investors aiming to grow their passive income portfolios.
  • 🏗️ Construction Loans: Custom lending for new construction or large-scale property transformations.

Our Lending Process

  • 📍 Deal Identification: Locate a viable investment property with potential.
  • 📝 Submit Your Request: Provide basic deal information: address, budget, and scope.
  • 📊 Quick Assessment: We analyze your property, projected ARV, and overall feasibility.
  • ✅ Pre-Approval: Receive a loan proposal within 24–48 hours.
  • ✍️ Closing: Sign, fund, and begin your project with confidence.
  • 🔧 Project Execution: Complete renovations while we coordinate draw disbursements.
  • 📈 Refinance or Sell: Exit via sale or rental conversion and move forward with your next deal.
